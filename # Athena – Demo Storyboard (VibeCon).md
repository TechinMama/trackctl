# Athena – Demo Storyboard (VibeCon)

## Demo Goal
Demonstrate calm, confident sports intelligence powered by AI—focused on awareness, clarity, and strategy.

Core message: Athena helps users follow the sport continuously, not just check isolated results.

---

## Scene 1: Home Feed (30s)
Show:
- Competitive weekend summary
- Upcoming major event
- Recent performance highlight
- Top 3 storylines module

Narration:
“Athena shows what matters in sport right now—and why.”

---

## Scene 2: Athlete Following (30s)
Show:
- Athlete profile
- Follow action
- AI momentum explanation

Narration:
“Athena treats athletes as evolving competitive narratives.”

---

## Scene 3: Event + Where to Watch (30s)
Show:
- Event overview
- Broadcast info
- Localized start time

Narration:
“Understanding is only useful if you can act on it.”

On-screen note:
"Broadcast info based on publicly available schedules and may vary by region."

---

## Scene 4: AI Performance Insight (45s)
Show:
- Raw result
- AI explanation card
- Historical/ranking context citation line

Narration:
“Athena explains significance without speculation.”

---

## Scene 5: Notification (15s)
Show:
- Athlete competing today alert

Narration:
“Notifications are selective, intentional, and relevant.”

---

## Demo Data Sources (Mention Briefly)
- Historical context: World Athletics stats zone
- Ranking movement: FloTrack rankings
- Results context: Track and Field News major results links
- Olympic event framing: LA28 athletics discipline list

---

## Close (10s)
“Athena is strategic intelligence for modern sport.”
